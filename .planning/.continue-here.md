## Critical Anti-Patterns (do NOT repeat these)
- SYNC I/O IN ASYNC DEF: sync Ollama/ChromaDB/pycti calls inside async def block uvicorn.
  Fix: asyncio.to_thread(sync_fn, args) or extract to sync helper + run_in_executor.
- UNCAPPED SINK ARGS: integer query params (n_results, limit, etc.) passed to external
  clients need max clamp. Validate ALL params at trust boundary, not just strings.
